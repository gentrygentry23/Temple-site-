OUR STORY

A Charming Hidden Gem
in Historic Dunnellon

Nestled in the heart of historic downtown Dunnellon, Sweeties Cafe and Tea House is a locally loved treasure where time slows down and every detail tells a story.

We’re community-centered, pouring our hearts into handmade comfort food, elegant tea service, and creating a space where neighbors become friends. Whether you’re relaxing indoors surrounded by vintage charm or unwinding on our garden patio, you’ll find the perfect spot to savor the moment.

From our famous homemade quiche and tomato pie to our curated selection of fine teas served in delicate china, everything is crafted with love and a touch of vintage elegance.

SIGNATURE EXPERIENCE

The Premium
High Tea Service

Step into elegance with our reservation-only afternoon tea experience. Served on traditional three-tiered stands with fine china, crystal, and sterling silver.

Begin with a selection of delicate finger sandwiches, followed by freshly baked warm scones served with house-made Devonshire cream and seasonal preserves. Conclude with an array of petit fours and French pastries, all paired with your choice of premium loose-leaf teas from around the world.
