Our Story

Taste of Home, Made with Love

At SWEET BOTTOM POT, we bring you the authentic taste of Nigerian cuisine straight from our kitchen to your table. Every pot tells a story of tradition, culture, and the warmth of Nigerian hospitality.

Founded in the heart of Port Harcourt, we specialize in freshly prepared local meals that remind you of mama's cooking. From the rich, velvety Afang soup of Akwa Ibom to the spicy Fisherman soup of the Niger Delta, every dish is crafted with premium ingredients and time-honored recipes.
